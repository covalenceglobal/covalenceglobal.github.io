Events

All of our events are climate-related and action-oriented. They strive to make a positive impact on topics including science/innovation, climate change breakthroughs, environmental awareness, environmental policy, new economic paradigms, etc, and reinforce our mission to foster "covalent bonds." If you are interested in partnering with us on an event, please fill out this form.


Covalence Global x Ice-Breaker's Youth Climate Change Conference

Covalence Global x Ice Breaker will be hosting a 3-day webinar on August 12, 13, and 14 from 11:00 am EST - 1:00 pm EST. This series is completely youth led and encourages participants and presenters to meet the climate change challenges of their time.

We are looking for participants who are passionate about climate change and willing to learn more. There will be a range of activities, collaborative discussions, and networking opportunities involved.

We are also looking for presenters who are passionate about climate change and its relation to justice, policy, economics, science/technology, etc. Presentations will range 15-20 minutes, followed by discussion in small breakout rooms.

To register for the event and/or sign up to present, you can fill out this form. All participants and presenters will receive a certificate of completion after attending the event. The deadline for presenter sign-ups is August 1st, and participants may register as late as August 12th. Even if you cannot attend the full event, we encourage you to register and Zoom-in when your time permits! Additionally, if you have any fellow youth leaders who would be interested in joining, please invite them to register.


Covalence Global x Youth Pawa - Ocean Conservation and Youth Advocacy

Covalence is partnering with Youth Pawa to organize two meetings in the month of September. Our events will address major ocean conservation and youth advocacy issues. Please check again for updates.
