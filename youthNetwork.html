Daria Nastasia - The George Washington University, Washington DC, USA

Darien Castro - Quito, Ecuador

"As a biologist, Darien has experience in research projects about ecological patterns and conservation programs that focus on direct effects of big scale phenomenons like climate change, deforestation process, and anthropogenic activities in Amazon and Andean region. He is the first author of two articles related to Indigenous resilience against Climate Change as multidisciplinary research with WHO collaborator Giulia Gasparri (g.gasparri96@gmail.com) for IJEPH (International Journal of Environment and Public Health) that explains the strategies and mechanisms that Indigenous communities from Amazon and South East rainforest have used during critical situations such as the COVID-19 outbreak and natural disasters as consequences of Climate Change (ex. floods, extreme drought seasons, soil erosion, wildfires ). The other article is based on COVID-19 distribution and ethnobiological geography in Amazon communities with the geographer Pablo Ochoa from Minnesota State University." Contact: +593 995926856
