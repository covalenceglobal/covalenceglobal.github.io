About Covalence - SF

Previously, Covalence was a locally focused organization. We worked to purify the Bay Area's waters through weekly beach cleanups. However, our focal point has shifted to a global mission. This is how Covalence - Global was born!

About Covalence - Global

Covalence's purpose is to foster "covalent bonds" by building a community of fellow environmentalists that strive to make a positive difference in climate change. Our mission is to inform people about climate change, inspire them to take action, and ultimately lead a global movement.

Our Logo

Firstly, we added the earth, our home, to emphasize what we are fighting for. We then included a group of "covalent" people wrapped around the planet, protecting it from the dangers of an increase in greenhouse gas emissions; we wanted to demonstrate how we are working to build an international network of unique and authentic people.
